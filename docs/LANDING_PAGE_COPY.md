## Problem
Good developer content is hard to make consistently.

Turning a coding concept into something short, clear, and visual usually means jumping between idea notes, scripting, storyboarding, video tools, and posting prep. That takes time, costs money, and often produces content that is too technical, too text-heavy, or not strong enough to post.

## Solution
CodeToons AI gives you a review-first workflow for coding explainers.

Start with a topic. Generate the idea, script, and storyboard. Review and edit the concept before spending on video generation. Then produce a short animated explainer and package it with platform-ready posting copy.

This is built for fast iteration, tighter creative control, and fewer wasted paid generations.

## How It Works
1. Enter a coding topic.
2. Generate the concept, script, and storyboard.
3. Review and edit before paid video generation.
4. Generate the final short animated video.
5. Export manual posting packages for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
